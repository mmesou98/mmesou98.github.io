MY STORY

I am Mmesoma Mary Nnaoma. I'm 19 going on 20. Number 5 amongst 7 children (yeah, seven children, lol).

I love to eat No matter how emotional I get, I never forget or joke with my food. Now, dont get it twisted. I don't eat too much, but I just don't joke with my meals.

I sing, I'm a chorister in my church choir. And I dance too.

I so love to skip. I mean the skipping rope thingy. Or, let me rather say I love to jump ropes.

I draw sometimes, act sometimes too (with myself alone, hehe).

I write so many times; I have lots of short stories, essays, written thoughts on my scrapbooks that only I, a few friends and my family members have read; many of which I have even lost.

Most of all, I love always;both God and man.

And yeah, lest I forget, I talk alot sometimes, but that after I've come to know you well and feel super comfortable around you because I'm super introvert.

Phew!! You know so much about me already. Now to the mundane stuffs.

Well in the year 2009, I graduated from Ojodu Primary School II. In 2012, I graduated from Ojodu Junior High School II. And in 2015, I graduated fron Babs Fafunwa Millenium Senior Grammar School, from science class. And till this time, like most my age and similar profile, I had only wanted be a medical doctor and nothing more.

I'm an active person evident in my crafts and multi-tasking. I had only waited for JAMB and opportunity for higher institution till April 2016 when I joined Digital Systems Connectivities Services; a financial e-channel services provider to banks and other financial institutions as an office assistant. And that's where and when my new quest to be tech savvy was born.

NOW, merging my aspirations, I dream night and day (yes, I close my eye everyday and see myself in this state) and see myself celebrated as the medical doctor who developed a technological ecosystem that simplifies and makes affordable quality health care services in Nigeria. I don't know how I would achieve this, but I know one thing.

I MUST TRY!
